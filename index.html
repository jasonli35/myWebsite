My favorite hobby are playing badminton and watching films. I just graduated from Galileo High School and will study computer engineering at UC San Diego.

My goal is to attend college to get a B.S. degree in computer science or engineering. I want to be a computer software and hardware engineer because I have enjoyed math, physics, and computer science the most throughout high school. Even before high school, I liked engineering. As a kid, I loved to play with lego. When I was in elementary school, I disassembled broken electronics, such as a radio, cassette player, cell phone, and telephone, and reassembled them because I liked to study how the circuits were connected. I have not made any of these broken electronics work, but I always told myself that I have to fix at least one of these electronic devices when I grow up. During my two and a half years of computer science classes in high school, I also enjoyed programming.

I am currently a student at Dev-Mission, where I learn technology skills in IoT (Internet of Things), IT Essentials (hardware and software), coding in HTML/CSS and Javascript and Critical Career Skills.
